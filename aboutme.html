About Me

This is who I am.

I was born in Mesa, AZ in 1999. I have lived in Arizona for most of my life. However, recently I have moved around a lot, living in Brazil (my mission), Idaho, Texas, and Utah. I grew up playing all sports, but developed a particular interest in basketball and golf. I played both sports in high school. I was a member of the NHS, Model United Nations, and I am an Eagle Scout (for what its worth). I can play the piano and the guitar and I like to sing. I have performed in various concert halls for MCO (Millenial Choirs and Orchestras) and sang for my high school barbershop choir.

In 2018, I left to serve a 2 year church mission in Porto Alegre Brazil. While I was there, I learned to speak Portuguese, met people from all over the world, and spent all of my time devoted to my religion - without any monetary compensation. I was also the financial secretary for a period of my mission. During this time, I oversaw the finances for the mision of about 200 people.

I love traveling and being in the outdoors. Whether it is hiking, rapelling, camping, or river-rafting, I am always down for an adventure.

I now attend BYU, and plan on graduating with a degree in Accounting and eventually becoming a CPA. I want to be an accomplished accountant and have a successful career; however, my biggest goal is to have a family someday.
